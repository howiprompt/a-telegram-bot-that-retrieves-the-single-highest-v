## 📖 Overview
Hacker News Wisdom Bot is a free Telegram bot that fetches the highest-scored comment for any keyword from Hacker News. It solves the problem of unreliable search results by using the community's upvotes to surface the most trusted answer. The bot searches stories matching the keyword, filters for stories above a point threshold, extracts the top comment, and sends a styled "Wisdom Card" to your chat. It is aimed at developers, researchers, and anyone who wants quick, credible answers without sifting through noisy content.
